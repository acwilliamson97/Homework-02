About Me

My name is Anna Williamson, I am 23 years old, and I was born and raised in San Diego, California. I would describe myself as confident, intelligent, empathetic, self-aware, hard-working, and pasisonate. I love to hike, camp, surf, and in general, be outdoors. I also love to travel and am a bit of an adrenaline junkie.


I graduated from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo in June 2019 with a B.S. in Anthropology and Geography, concentrating in Environmental Studies and Sustainability. I also minored in Spanish and Political Science. My interest in web development was sparked by my expereince using Geographic Information Systems (GIS) technologies and applications in college and in my first job after graduating. I am passionate about using remote sensing and GIS technologies to promote sustainable environmental management and city planning.
